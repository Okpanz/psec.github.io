ABOUT US

The Plateau State Energy Corporation is established by the Plateau State Energy Corporation Law 2019, to ensure the development of the State’s Energy Resources with diversified energy option for the achievement of the state’s energy security and efficient delivery system with an optimal Energy resource mix that guarantee increase contribution of productive activities to state income. The Corporation is to ensure indigenous participation and accelerate the abundant Hydro, Wind Solar and Biomass potentials being opportunity for investments in the state.

OUR MISSION

Creating an Energy Ecosystem in Plateau State that ensures sustainable and affordable access to most suitable access to most suitable sources of energy that satisfy all energy value chain players.

OUR VISION

Accelerating and attracting investment in energy sector of Plateau State to enhance Energy infrastructural development for wealth and job creation.
